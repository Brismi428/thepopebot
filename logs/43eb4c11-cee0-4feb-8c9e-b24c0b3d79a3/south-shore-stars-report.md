## Executive Summary

South Shore Stars School & Learning Center is a private nonprofit day school in Weymouth, Massachusetts, serving students in grades 1-8 with dyslexia and other language-based learning differences. The school opened in September 2025 and has received full Chapter 766 approval from the Massachusetts Department of Elementary and Secondary Education (DESE), enabling partnerships with public school systems and access to publicly funded educational support. The school emphasizes evidence-based instruction, small class sizes, Orton-Gillingham certified teachers, and individualized support in a structured, supportive environment.

**Key Highlights:**
- Specialized instruction for students with dyslexia and language-based learning differences
- Grades 1-8 (K-8 DESE approved as of January 2026)
- Orton-Gillingham certified teachers
- Small class sizes and low student-to-teacher ratios
- Chapter 766 approved (eligible for public funding)
- Located at 163 Libbey Parkway, Weymouth, MA 02189

### Ideal Student Profile

**Source:** https://southshorestars.org/stars-school-learning-center/

The school may be a good fit if a child:
- Is bright but struggles with reading, writing, or language
- Has dyslexia or a language-based learning difference
- Has ADHD as a co-occurring challenge
- Needs structured, multi-sensory instruction
- Is not thriving in a traditional classroom

The school clarifies this may NOT be the right fit if:
- The child does not have language-based learning needs
- Parents are looking for a general enrichment program
- The child is best served in a large, traditional classroom
